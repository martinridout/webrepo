Simulation of fungal colony growth

This applet, written by David Ridout, simulates the inhibition model.

Black dots represent spores of the organism of interest. Red dots represent inhibitory organisms. Each type of spore is distributed randomly across the dish. A black spore is unable to germinate and grow if the distance to the nearest red spore is less than the inhibition distance.

You can vary the number of each type of spore and the inhibition distance. Note that the Run button doesn't work while the spores are growing.
