Funding

Currently, the project is funded through a BBSRC project grant (BB/H012982) awarded to Mick Tuite (Biosciences), Tobias von der Haar (Biosciences), Byron Morgan (SMSAS) and Martin Ridout (SMSAS), supporting the work of postdocs Wes Naeimi (Biosciences) and Vasileios Giagos (SMSAS). This grant runs from 2010-2013.

An EPSRC doctoral training grant supported Karen Palmer, who completed her PhD thesis "Stochastic Models for Cell Biology" in 2009.

Initial funding for the project was through a BBSRC project grant (96/E18382) awarded to Mick Tuite (Biosciences), Byron Morgan (SMSAS) and Martin Ridout (SMSAS), supporting the work of postdocs Lee Byrne (Biosciences) and Diana Cole (SMSAS). This A-graded grant ran from 2003-2005.
