About this project

These web pages describe a collaborative project between the School of Biosciences and the School of Mathematics, Statistics and Actuarial Science (SMSAS) at the University of Kent.

Our objective is to use stochastic modelling techniques to improve our understanding of prion proteins in the yeast Saccharomyces cerevisiae. These pages describe some of the biological background to the work and outline the models that we have developed.
