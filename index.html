I am Emeritus Professor of Applied Statistics within Mathematics, Statistics and Actuarial Science at the University of Kent, UK, where I also have an official home page.

I retired in 2019 and am no longer teaching or supervising research students.

Before moving to the University of Kent, I worked as a statistical consultant in agricultural and horticultural research, most recently at East Malling Research, which is now part of Niab.

I have broad interests in applied statistics, stochastic modelling and statistical computing. Much of my work has focused on applications in ecology and bioscience.
